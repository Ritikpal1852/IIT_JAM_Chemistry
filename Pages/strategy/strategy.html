TIPS TO REMEMBER AND FOLLOW DURING PREPARATION
  1. Understand the previous year questions pattern, Practice previous year questions during preparation
  2. Try to understand the concepts, practice tests and revise regularly.
  3. Most of the questions in JAM are conceptual & numerical, so you must try to improve your solving ability & time management to score a good rank.
  4. Your plan should include syllabus completion, revision, and practicing tests.
  5. First step in the preparation is to start with easy topic and important one. Toppers suggestion is to begin with basic topic.
  6. Make a revision notes for each topic that should cover all important definition, formulas, differences, etc.
  7. Attempt the tests for each topic and subject to know your performance and Clear all your doubts during your preparation.
REVISE THE JAM PREPARATION
  1. It is not just sufficient enough to prepare revision notes, but also to revise them regularly.
  2. Read the revision notes on a weekly basis to recall the concepts of prepared subjects.
  3. Each time you revise the same topic/subject, your revision time will reduce to recall the concepts.
