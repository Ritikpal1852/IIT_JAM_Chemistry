With coaching Or Without coaching

If you attend a coaching centre, you can interact with like-minded people, understand their perspectives, take tips from their learning styles, improve your knowledge and be abreast with latest updates. The competitive environment will keep up your motivation levels. Inputs from faculty will help grasp more knowledge within limited period of time. Taking tests that they conduct regularly will help you improve your speed and ability to handle different types of questions. As per some successful JAM aspirants, Coaching study material is sufficient for JAM/M.Sc entrance syllabus

The general perception is that coaching is mandatory for cracking IIT-JAM exam. However, every year there are candidates, who ace the exam without coaching. So, if you are in the same category of non-coaching candidates then do not worry about the entrance test and make a note of these important tips. (1) It is very important to understand the exam and what is expected out of a candidate via it. To get this, the best sources is the exam pattern and syllabus of JAM 2022. (2) Candidates should check the syllabus and divide it into strong and weak topics. Since the exam is here, this is the time to make strong subjects stronger. (3) JAM question paper is prepared in such a manner that it checks candidateâ€™s basic knowledge of the subject, as well as, the depth of it. Hence, it is important to go back and revise the basics learned in your graduation hence, one should also revise BSc coursework thoroughly. (4) There is no better source to prepare for an examination, other than previous year question paper and mock test. So, do make sure to solve as many questions paper and mock test as one can. Maintain a regular study schedule, Refer standard books, Dedication, Self Motivation and belive in yourself

Four types of Aspirants: Strategy

Time > 1 Year- Most important thing if your are aiming for double digit rank then you need to read all topics (major+mino) from you syllabus which are prescribed, go thoroughly. Have some patience ,there are some topics deserve much more time(6-7 times reading) so in that case patience is the key. Next thing is to improve your problem solving ability , some books like claydon in organic chemistry is a wonderful book which will help you to do so in chemistry ( it'll also help you to brush up your basic concepts). Last solve as many test-series as you can, preferebly 5-6 test series and around 100 test papers in total in last 4-5 months ,that playes the most important role in acheiving double digit rank. Many of them are provided freely too. Remeber, Revision is the most important thing in preparation. The more you revise, the more marks you will get

Time = 1 Year- Stratergy is nearly same as above, just do thing fastly but don't compromise with quality. Narrow down your study material. Revision is the most important thing in preparation. The more you revise, the more marks you will get

Time â‰ˆ 6 months- First of all, try to complete major major topics of syllabus. After that the focus is upon solved papers and previous year question papers of IIT JAM Chemistry. Solve/Analyse all previous year questions, but more importantly analyse what number of questions asked from a particular branch and prepare accordingly. Next thing is to Make short notes by yourself, write everything in it which you think is important. Revise them daily, twice on Sundays. Don't touch whole new topics in last 1-2 months , solve as many tests as you can in last 3-4 months. After solving tests analyse your mistakes and your weak topics and work on them.

Time < 3-4 months- It's really important that you find scoring topics and go after them. Pick up any coaching material and solve those jam related problems. Don't try to cover all syllabus, make 5 subjects strong and solve their Unit Tests also.
